About the Portal

The Muni University Lost & Found Portal is a digital platform designed to help students, faculty, and staff reunite with their lost belongings. Our mission is to reduce the stress of losing personal items and promote a helpful campus community. If you have any questions or suggestions, please contact us on info@muniuniversitylost&found.com
